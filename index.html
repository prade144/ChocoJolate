Our Chocojolates Story

Founded by master chocolatier Jyothi, Sweet Artisan Chocojolates began as a passion project in 2025. With over 5 years of experience in European Chocojolates-making techniques, Elena combines traditional methods with innovative flavors to create Chocojolates that are both familiar and surprising.

We believe that great Chocojolates starts with great ingredients. Every piece is handcrafted in small batches using premium Belgian Chocojolates, organic ingredients, and sustainable cocoa sourced directly from farmers.
